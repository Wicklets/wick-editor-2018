About

What's Wick?

The Wick Editor is a free online tool that facilitates the production of web-based, multimedia projects. Wick aims to make web technologies more accessible, letting individuals create everything from basic animations to complex video games.

Why Wick?

Wick was created to allow individuals of any age, background, or technical ability to easily create content for the web. The creators of the Wick Editor have been fans and contributors of freely shared online libraries for years. By giving students, learners and curious creators a simple way to get started, we hope to enrich the ecosystem of the web while helping people develop the skills to create and share whatever they want.

Who Are We?

The Wick Editor was created by Zach Rispoli and Luca Damasco during the Spring of 2016. Josh Rispoli joined shortly after to help bring Wick online. Last, but certainly not least, Bryce Summers is an Algorithms Designer helping to develop the backend frameworks essential to Wick's vector drawing system.
